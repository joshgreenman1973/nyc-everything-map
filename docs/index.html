Read this before you rely on the map

This site was built with AI assistance. Quality checks that were done: boundary lookups were validated against 900 New York Police Department records and 880 311 records (99.8%+ agreement net of records the city itself labels differently); every elected official was checked against an official or maintained public source in June 2026; demographic figures were spot-checked against published American Community Survey tables. Quality checks that were not done: not every possible lookup has been human-reviewed, live feeds are shown as the city publishes them, school data carries the vintages noted in each section and zoned school assignments change. Verify anything consequential with the agency itself. Full methodology.
