Read this before you rely on the map

This site was built with AI assistance. Quality checks that were done: boundary lookups were validated against 900 New York Police Department records (99.8% agreement net of records the department itself labels differently) and 880 311 records; every elected official was checked against an official or maintained public source in June 2026; demographic figures were spot-checked against published American Community Survey tables. Quality checks that were not done: not every one of the thousands of possible lookups has been human-reviewed, live feeds (311, crime, property data) are shown as the city publishes them and zoned school assignments can change year to year. Verify anything consequential with the agency itself. Full methodology.
